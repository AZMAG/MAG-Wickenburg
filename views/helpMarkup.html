The markup tool provides tools for adding graphics to the map.

When the markup tool is active the pop-ups in the map will be inactivated. To identify a layer using pop-ups make sure the markup tools window is not active.

Add a graphic
  1. Choose a markup tool from the dropdown.
  2. Click the fill color box and choose a fill color.
  3. Slide the trasparency slider to choose the appropriate amount of opacity.
  4. Click the outline color box and choose a color for the graphic outline.
  5. Click the button to start drawing your graphic.
  6. If you chose Text as your tool, please enter the text input in the textbox and choose the appropriate font size.
  7. Simply click and drag to draw on the map.
  8. To undo the most recent graphic, click the button next to the add button.
Editing Graphics
  1. Click the button.
  2. Click a graphic.
  3. Click any of the points on the graphic and drag to manipulate the shape.
  4. To edit text, simply double click on a text graphic and enter new text.
Deleting Graphics
  1. Click the button.
  2. Click any of the added graphics.
