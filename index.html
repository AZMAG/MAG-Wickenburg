About the Wickenburg Zoning Viewer

The Wickenburg Zoning Viewer is an interactive mapping application that displays updated zoning information for the Town of Wickenburg.

Navigate around the map using your mouse.

  • To Pan (move the map), simply click and hold your mouse, then drag the map.
  • To Center and Zoom In on a location, double click the location.
  • To Zoom In using the mouse scroll wheel, scroll forward.
  • To Zoom Out using the mouse scroll wheel, scroll backward.
  • Select an area to Zoom to by holding down the shift key while drawing a box.
  • Move the map by holding the left mouse button while you drag.
  • Click on a map feature to reveal further information.

Data Source

  • Town of Wickenburg Zoning 2012
  • Parcel data provided by the Maricopa and Yavapai County Assessors Office
  • Floodways data from FEMA and The Flood Conrtrol District of Maricopa County
  • Aerial Imagery provided by ESRI last updated December 2012
  • Street map provided by ESRI last updated July 2012
